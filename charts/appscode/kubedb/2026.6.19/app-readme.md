KubeDB by AppsCode is a Kubernetes-native Database-as-a-Service platform for running production databases on Rancher-managed clusters. It enables platform teams to provision and operate popular engines such as PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, Redis, Kafka, Elasticsearch, and more using declarative Kubernetes workflows.

With KubeDB, teams can standardize database operations across on-prem, cloud, and air-gapped environments with built-in automation for day-2 tasks like scaling, upgrades, backup and restore, monitoring, and high availability while preserving governance and multi-tenant controls.
